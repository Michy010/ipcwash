About Us

The IPCWASH MUHAS organisation for students of Environmental Health is a non-profit organisation dedicated to promoting and advancing the principles of Infections Prevention and Control(IPC) and Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene (WASH) among students pursuing studies in environmental health.
